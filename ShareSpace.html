How It Works

Six simple steps from your first visit to a meaningful conversation.

1
Create an Account or Enter as Guest

Sign up with a username and password, or proceed anonymously as a guest. Guests receive a random nickname (e.g., "CalmRiver42") and a temporary session ID ‚Äî no personal data collected.

2
Complete Onboarding

Learn about chat rules, data privacy policies, and session types. Set your preferences ‚Äî topics of concern (anxiety, relationships, work stress), preferred language, and emotional intensity level.

3
Choose a Session Type

Select between a free volunteer listener for general emotional support, or a paid certified mentor / therapist for deeper professional guidance.

4
Pick a Listener or Quick Match

Browse listener profiles with ratings, specialties, and availability. Or use Quick Match to be paired instantly with the best available listener for your needs.

5
Start Your Chat

Enter the private, encrypted chat room. Share freely ‚Äî there are no cameras, no recordings, no judgment. AI prompts gently help if the conversation stalls. You can end, block, or report at any time.

6
Leave Feeling Lighter

After the session, rate your experience anonymously. Your session data auto-deletes based on your privacy settings. Come back whenever you need to be heard.

Privacy & Data Policy

Your privacy is our highest priority. Here is exactly how we handle your data.

üîí Anonymous by Design

ShareSpace was built anonymity-first. Guest sessions require zero personal information ‚Äî you are assigned a random nickname and a temporary session ID that expires after your chat ends. Registered accounts require only a username and password; no email is mandatory.

üóÑÔ∏è Data Retention

Guest chat transcripts are permanently deleted the moment a session ends. Registered users may optionally save session summaries, which are stored encrypted and can be deleted at any time from Settings. We never store voice, video, or biometric data ‚Äî because we never collect it.

üîê Encryption

All communications travel over TLS 1.3. Chat messages are end-to-end encrypted between participants. Our servers handle routing only ‚Äî listeners and mentors cannot see your account details.

üö´ What We Never Do

We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers. We never use your chat content to train AI models without explicit opt-in consent. We never allow employers, governments, or third parties to access your sessions without a valid legal order.

‚öñÔ∏è Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or permanently delete all data associated with your account. Submit a data request from the Help page and we will respond within 48 hours.

üìû Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@sharespace.io ‚Äî we respond within 2 business days.

Help Center

Find answers to common questions below, or contact our support team.

How do I start a chat anonymously? +
Click "Continue as Guest" on the homepage. You'll receive a random nickname and temporary session ID instantly ‚Äî no sign-up required.
Can listeners see my real name? +
Never. Listeners only see your anonymous nickname. Registered users control their display name separately from their account username.
How do I become a listener? +
Apply via the "Become a Listener" section of your dashboard. Complete our empathy training program (approx. 4 hours) and pass a short assessment to go live.
What if my mentor is unhelpful? +
You can end the session at any time and request a full refund from the session summary screen. No questions asked ‚Äî your wellbeing comes first.
Are chats recorded? +
Guest chats are never stored. Registered users can optionally save encrypted summaries. We never record audio or video ‚Äî the platform is text-only.
How do I delete my account? +
Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Delete Account. This permanently erases all your data within 24 hours. This action cannot be undone.
What are the chat rules? +
No hate speech, no harassment, no sharing personal contact info, no solicitation. Listeners are here to support ‚Äî not to provide medical diagnoses. Any violations can be reported in-chat.
